This implementation guide is meant to facilitate operationalization of the technical specifications and implementation guidance issued by the World Health Organization (WHO) for Digital Documentation of COVID-19 Certificates: Vaccination Status (DDCC:VS) and an accompanying annexed data dictionary in spreadsheet format outlining the DDCC:VS core data set requirements for the continuity of care and proof of vaccination scenarios.

The following are references included in the DDCC:VS - Technical specifications and implementation guidance document, organized by section and topic. Standards referenced are also included in Dependencies.
