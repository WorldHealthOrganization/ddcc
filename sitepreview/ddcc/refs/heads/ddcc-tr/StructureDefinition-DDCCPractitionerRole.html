Resource Profile: DDCC Practitioner Role

Defining URL:http://worldhealthorganization.github.io/ddcc/StructureDefinition/DDCCPractitionerRole
Version:0.3.1
Name:DDCCPractitionerRole
Title:DDCC Practitioner Role
Status:Active as of 1/25/22, 2:32 PM
Definition:

Association of an DDCC Practitioner to a managing DDCC Organization, indicating that an DDCC Practitioner is authorized by the public health authority (PHA) to vaccinate an DDCC Patient.

Publisher:WHO
Source Resource:XML / JSON / Turtle

The official URL for this profile is: 

http://worldhealthorganization.github.io/ddcc/StructureDefinition/DDCCPractitionerRole
